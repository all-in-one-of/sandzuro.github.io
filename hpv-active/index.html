Hyperverse is a new full-immersion VR.
Travel alone or with friends.
Join the new era of all-senses video games.
Hyperverse produces full-immersion free roam VR parks
We are a group of like-minded individuals on a path to bring a completely new way of entertainment into this reality. From countless new realities.
It’s not 5D
It’s endless dimensions, squared. Hyperverse have created a portal to the new worlds to be witnessed alone or with friends. We have made the interdimensional travel available to everybody. And safe (allegedly).
Here is what you get: everything
After arriving at one of our locations, you pick the world you want to witness. You get equipped and then instructed by our pioneer guides on how to use the equipment. And then you get to see a completely new universe. Our legal team advised us to remind that you also get to return back (allegedly).
The tech we have crafted to allow for the travel is impressive
You are free. No wires attach you like an umbilical cord to old boring existence. So you’re on your own. You get to use your whole body as a controller. And you will need that. Every gesture counts.
In short — there is no easy way to describe what we have achieved
Except for the word “unachievable”. You will feel the winds and rains of alien atmospheres. Use tools. Interact with other players without occlusion — so get as close as you have to.
As pioneers of new dimensions you might require assistance from your friends or fellow explorers
Please be reminded that cooperation is not legally required, but is strongly encouraged.
The worlds you can access are diverse
Some are wild and some are tame. Well, tamer. Some are simpler and some are mind-boggling. They have one thing in common — none of them are boring. Because we at Hyperverse do not do boring. Our goal was to bring the future tech into today. To build what will become the magical lantern of cyberpunk. To open up the path to the new era of all-in full-sensory VR entertainment.
And we did that
So join us in getting to the next stage. It’s fun. No robot uprisings or malfunctioning AI becoming sentient (allegedly).
