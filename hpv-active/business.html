Hyperverse is an aggregated platform for high-end location-based VR entertainment

Our equipment lets you turn a vacant space of virtually any shape and size into a portal to the worlds unseen.

Everything needed for the transformation is included: from fully integrated hardware with high-precision positional tracking to a catalog of games created specifically for the hardware.

We are on a path to change location-based entertainment(or LBE, for short). To create a new path to the virtual worlds, where self-expression is self-exploration. Come and join us on this pioneer trail.

Emotions are king

The sense of wonder comes from suspension of disbelief. The only way to deal with that is by turning technology into the miracle that “just works”. To immerse a player into fantasy. Immersion creates engagement.

So naturally the Hyperverse experience is built around casting magic through technology. It includes free roaming with unlimited space of any shapes: hallways, pathways, variable ceiling heights. The equipment provides precise body tracking. It allows for advanced physical interactions with virtual and real objects, such as haptic feedback and weather systems.

All the tech works to bring to life our catalog of VR content. And the content is picked and adapted to support our values and goals, and to get the most out of our hardware.

Here are some magical facts and numbers:
  • Movement precision under 1 cm with less than 10 ms latency (which is really impressive);
  • Cost-effective inside-out tracking. So, no cameras on the walls, just one camera on each player;
  • Which means — unlimited multiplayer, because there’s no optical occlusion;
  • With precise hands and fingers tracking;
  • External stimuli such as haptic feedback and weather systems fully synced with the in-game events;
The miracle comes with a sensible price tag, as well as our help, support, and participation both during installation and during operation.
Through the opening of the box
The Hyperverse offer is as all-encompassing as the virtual worlds themselves. We will be your partner from the beginning to the endless success.
Here’s what comes with our single offer:
  • Branding and design guidelines for the gaming space;
  • Visitors’ logistics workflows and experience handbooks;
  • All-around technological setup with installation, on-site and off-site support;
  • Gaming content, adapted for the location both technically and artistically;
  • Access to the Hyperverse software and content repository;
Player’s equipment:
  • Oculus Rift
  • VR ready backpack
  • Oculus Touch
  • Leap Motion
The grand battle

Hyperverse is here to battle the gaps on the LBE market. We give experience operators what is needed to create the best LBE experience. We provide simple, easy to integrate and easy to adopt SDKs and libraries to content creators. And then we connect together every party involved.

Our goal is simple — we want to make travel to the worlds unseen easy, convenient and yet wondrous.
